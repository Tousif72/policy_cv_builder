Account Management

Delete Your Account & Data

You have the right to permanently delete your Resumind account and all associated personal data at any time. This right is provided in accordance with GDPR (Article 17), CCPA, and Google Play Developer Policy.

⚠️ Important — please read before submitting:
Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. Once processed, the following data will be completely and permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
📧 Email address 📄 All resumes & CVs ✉️ Cover letters ⚡ AI usage history 🔑 Account credentials ☁️ Cloud sync data 📊 Score & match results
How it works
  1. Click the "Delete My Account Now" button below and follow the 2-step confirmation.
  2. Enter your registered email address and tick the confirmation checkbox.
  3. Your account and all data are deleted immediately — no waiting required.
  4. You can also delete directly from the app: Settings → Delete Account.
  5. Active Premium subscriptions must be cancelled separately via Google Play Store settings before requesting deletion.
