Purpose: Powers AI features including Improve Summary, Improve Bullets, Resume Score, and Job Match.

Purpose: Powers the Cover Letter generation feature.
Data sent: Resume text and job role context — only when you actively generate a cover letter.

Purpose: Collects anonymised crash reports to help us detect and fix app stability issues.
Data sent: Stack traces, device model, OS version, app version. No resume content is included.

Our Own Server

Purpose: Stores your account (email + device ID), subscription status, AI usage counts, and optionally your resumes (Cloud Sync).

Security: All communication over HTTPS/TLS. Data encrypted at rest. Access controlled by Bearer token authentication.

Section 6

Advertising

⭐ Premium Users — No Ads

If you subscribe to Resumind Premium, no advertisements are shown to you at any time, in any part of the app.

Free users see ads served by Google AdMob. Ads may appear as:

  • Banner ads — shown at the bottom of certain screens
  • Interstitial ads — full-screen ads shown between actions (e.g. after navigating between resumes)
  • Rewarded ads — optional ads you can watch voluntarily in exchange for bonus AI credits

Consent for Personalised Ads (GDPR / CCPA)

When you first open Resumind in a supported region (EU/EEA, UK, California), we display a consent dialogue powered by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). You can:

  • Accept personalised ads — Google uses your Advertising ID to show ads relevant to your interests.
  • Decline personalised ads — you still see ads, but they are not targeted to your interests. Google uses contextual signals only.

You can change this choice at any time: Settings → Legal → Manage Ad Preferences.

Advertising ID

You can reset or opt out of ad personalisation entirely in your Android device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID or Delete Advertising ID (Android 12+).

Section 7

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

All resumes and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's Room database. This data is protected by Android's application sandboxing — no other app can access it. It is deleted when you uninstall Resumind or clear app data.

Cloud Storage (Optional)

When you sign in with your email, your resumes are automatically backed up to our secure server so you can access them from any device. This sync is enabled by signing in and disabled by signing out (your local data is cleared on sign-out).

Security Measures

  • Transport security: All communication between the app and our server uses HTTPS / TLS 1.2+. Plain HTTP is never used in production.
  • Authentication: Every API request is authenticated with a unique Bearer token tied to your device and email. Tokens are never transmitted over unencrypted connections.
  • Data isolation: Your resumes are scoped to your user ID at the database level. Other users cannot access your data.
  • Encryption at rest: Data on our server is stored with encryption at the storage layer.
  • AI key security: API keys used to call AI services are held in server-side environment variables. They are never embedded in the Android app binary or stored on your device.
  • Principle of least privilege: Our server database user has only the permissions required for the app to function. No unrestricted database access.
⚠️ No System Is Perfect

While we implement industry-standard security practices, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

Regional Rights

European Union / EEA / UK — GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR:

  • Right to Restrict Processing — You can ask us to limit how we use your data while a dispute is resolved.
  • Right to Object — You can object to processing based on our legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent (e.g. personalised ads), you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint — You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. For a list of EU supervisory authorities, visit edpb.europa.eu.

California, USA — CCPA / CPRA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grant you additional rights:

  • Right to Know — Categories and specific pieces of personal information collected.
  • Right to Delete — Request deletion of personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale / Sharing — We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioural advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Right to Correct — Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Data — We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

California residents may submit requests by emailing vizotechno@gmail.com with the subject "CCPA Privacy Request".

Canada — PIPEDA

Canadian users have rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), including the right to access and correct personal information and to withdraw consent. Contact us at vizotechno@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Brazil — LGPD

Users in Brazil have rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), including the right to access, correct, delete, and port personal data, and to revoke consent. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Australia — Privacy Act 1988

Australian users have rights under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.

India — DPDP Act 2023

Users in India have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, including the right to access information about data processing, the right to correction and erasure, and the right to nominate a representative. Contact us to exercise these rights.
